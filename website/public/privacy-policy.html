1. Privacy Policy

We Never Collect Your Data: We do not track, monitor, or collect any personal, device, or usage data. Your privacy is absolute.

1.1 Data Collection and Usage

We maintain a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No Tracking: We never track, monitor, or collect any personal, device, or usage data
  • No Storage: The app functions without storing, analyzing, or profiling your activity
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics tools to monitor user behavior
  • No User Profiles: We do not create profiles based on your usage patterns
  • Simply Put: No tracking of any kind

1.2 Image Processing

Your images are processed with the utmost care and security:

  • Purpose: Uploaded images are used exclusively for AI generation purposes
  • AI Models: Processing is performed by trusted AI models:
    • Google Nano Banana
    • WAN 2.2 Fast Image-to-Video
  • Temporary Processing: Images are processed temporarily during generation and deleted immediately afterward
  • Local Storage Only: Generated results are stored only on your device and never on our servers
  • No Retention: We do not keep any copies of your uploaded or generated images

1.3 Camera Permission

The app requires camera access for the following purposes:

  • Photo Capture: To allow you to take photos directly within the app for AI processing
  • Real-Time Preview: To provide live camera preview before capturing images
  • Your Control: Camera access is only activated when you choose to use it
  • No Recording: We do not record video or audio without your explicit action
  • Permission Management: You can revoke camera access anytime through your device settings

1.4 Data Storage and Security

Our commitment to your security:

  • No Server Storage: Since no user or device data is collected, we do not store personal information on our servers
  • Encrypted Communication: All communication with third-party AI services is encrypted via HTTPS for security
  • Device-Only Storage: All your generated content remains exclusively on your device
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not backup your images to any cloud service

1.5 Third-Party Services

We work with the following trusted third-party services:

  • AI Processing: Google Nano Banana, WAN 2.2 Fast Image-to-Video
  • Payments & Subscriptions: Apple App Store / Google Play Store
  • Authentication (if applicable): Firebase
  • Important: These services process data according to their own privacy policies

1.6 Children's Privacy

Age Restrictions and Parental Guidance:

  • Not Recommended for Under 18: This app is not recommended for users under 18 years of age
  • Parental Supervision Required: For users under 18, parental guidance and supervision is mandatory
  • Under 13: We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13 without explicit parental consent
  • Parent Responsibility: Parents and guardians should monitor their children's use of the app
  • Account Deletion: Parents may request deletion of any data related to their children by contacting support

You have the following rights:
